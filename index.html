About Me

About Me

Hello everyone, let me introduce myself. My name would have been Sariowan Preisilia Veronica, commonly called "Prei". I would have been very happy to do sports, although sometimes only when the mood would come. At that time, I would have been a second-semester student of Informatics Engineering at Politeknik Negeri Manado. I would have been born in Amurang on February 2, 2006, and I would have found a strong interest in photography, fascinated by the beauty that would have been around us.

Through this personal page, I would have hoped to share the moments I would have captured daily as a student. Each day would have been filled with interesting lectures, idea-filled group discussions, and academic challenges that would have spurred creativity.

For me, being a student would not have just been about obtaining a degree, but also about exploring interests, developing skills, and shaping character. Every experience, both inside and outside the classroom, would have been a step towards personal growth and maturity.

I would have believed that the daily life of a student would have been a unique and valuable journey, where every moment would have provided a new lesson. And I would have been excited to continue exploring, learning, and growing during this journey.

Achievement Of Activities

Achievement Of Activities

The following are some moments of activities that I participated in during my lectures and assignments on campus:

KATRILI DANCE

I took part in the Katrili Dance on campus to appreciate Indonesian culture in commemoration of the 78th Heroes' Day and the 36th Polytechnic Anniversary, as an implementation of MBKM & MKWU.

TOMOHON SION OLD CHURCH

At that time, our group made a Citizenship Course assignment and we visited the GMIM SION Church in Tomohon City and this church was designated as a National Cultural Heritage Site which was founded in 1831.

TUUR MA'ASERING

We also visited the Tuur Ma'asering tourist attraction which is located in Tomohon City. This place has very beautiful natural views, this entrance ticket can later be exchanged for drinks typical of Minahasa local wisdom, namely Saguer and the famous Cap Tikus liquor.

TRAINING & CERTIFICATION

I took part in the SKKNI Training & Certification Activities from the Ministry of Communication and Information BPSDMP Manado, and I took the Junior Web Developer scheme.

SPIRITUAL BUILDING XXXI

I took part in Campus Spiritual Development Activities organized by the Student Activities Unit of the Christian Spiritual Bureau.

UKM MULTIMEDIA

At campus I also took part in the Multimedia Student Activity Unit to develop my interest and talent in liking the field of photography.
